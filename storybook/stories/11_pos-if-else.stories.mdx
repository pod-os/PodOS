## pos-if-else

Selects a template to render based on properties of the subject uri

<Canvas
  withSource="open">
  <Story
    name="pos-if-else"
  >
    {({uri}) => html`
    <pos-resource uri="${uri}">
      <pos-if-else>
        <template if-typeof="http://www.w3.org/ns/ldp#Container">Container</template>
        <template if-typeof="http://www.w3.org/ns/ldp#BasicContainer">Basic Container</template>
        <template else if-typeof="http://www.w3.org/ns/ldp#Resource">Resource and not a container</template>
        <template else>Not a resource or container</template>
      </pos-if-else>
</pos-resource>
    `}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
